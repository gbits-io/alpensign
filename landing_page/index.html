The Problem

Banks lose billions to fraud ‚Äî and their only evidence is their own logs.

When a client disputes a payment, the bank's defense rests on server logs they generated, stored, and control. That's self-serving evidence. Courts know it. Regulators know it. Fraudsters know it.

Self-Serving Evidence

Bank logs prove what the bank says happened. In a dispute, that's structurally weak ‚Äî the bank is both the record keeper and the interested party.

üí∏

Friendly Fraud

Clients who made legitimate purchases then dispute them. Buyer's remorse disguised as fraud. Banks absorb billions annually because they can't prove the client was present.

Weak Authentication Proof

SMS OTP, push notifications, app-based 2FA ‚Äî all controlled by the bank or dependent on Google's infrastructure. No independent, verifiable trail.

The Solution

Three layers of trust. Zero trust in intermediaries.

AlpenSign creates an independent evidence chain ‚Äî from verified hardware through verified identity to immutable proof of authorization.

1

Genesis Token ‚Äî Device is genuine

The Seeker's soulbound NFT proves the phone is real hardware, verified by SKR-staked Guardians through TEEPIN. No Google. No centralized attestation.

Soulbound NFT ¬∑ Non-transferable
2

Bank Credential ‚Äî Client is verified

The bank issues a credential via Solana Attestation Service to the Seeker wallet, binding the client relationship to the verified device. Revocable by the bank.

SAS Attestation ¬∑ Revocable
3

Transaction Seal ‚Äî Payment is authorized

Client reviews payment details on-device, confirms with biometric. The Seeker's secure element signs and the proof is posted to Solana. Permanent. Immutable.
