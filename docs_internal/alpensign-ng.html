The Problem

AI can produce anything.
Who proves a human was involved?

2026

AI drafts the contract

Law firms use LLMs to generate complex agreements. When a clause is disputed, opposing counsel argues no qualified lawyer meaningfully reviewed the AI's output.

2027

AI writes the diagnosis

Hospital AI systems analyze imaging and generate diagnostic reports. Insurers question whether a licensed physician engaged with the AI's finding before it was issued.

2028

AI files the report

Asset managers publish AI-assisted research to institutional clients. Regulators demand proof that named analysts exercised genuine judgment — not rubber-stamped AI output.

Today's answer is "trust us, a human reviewed it." That's the same self-serving evidence problem banks have had for decades. AlpenSign-NG makes the proof independent, cryptographic, and immutable.

How It Works

Three layers of independent proof

L1

Device Verified

Genuine hardware with tamper-resistant secure element, independently attested on-chain

L2

Person Credentialed

Professional credential issued by authority — bar admission, medical license, FINMA registration

L3

Decision Sealed

Biometric confirmation + document hash + session metadata, anchored on Solana as an immutable attestation

Professional reviews content

A lawyer, doctor, analyst, or authorized person reviews the AI-assisted document or decision on their verified device. AlpenSign-NG records session duration and interaction.

Biometric seal from secure element

The reviewer taps "Approve & Seal." Biometric authentication triggers the device's hardware-isolated signing environment. The private key never leaves the secure element.

Attestation posted on-chain

A Solana Attestation Service (SAS) credential is posted: document hash, professional credential reference, device attestation, timestamp. Only hashes on-chain — full data stays with the parties.

Evidence bundle generated

A self-contained, court-admissible evidence package — PDF + JSON + on-chain references — that any party can independently verify. No reliance on AlpenSign-NG systems.

Why Switzerland

The jurisdiction matters

Neutrality

Outside every major bloc

Not subject to US subpoena, EU jurisdiction shopping, or PRC data access. For multinationals, a Swiss attestation authority is uniquely neutral ground.

Legal Framework

ZertES + nDSG

Swiss electronic signatures law and the Federal Act on Data Protection provide a mature legal framework for cryptographic attestation with strong privacy guarantees.

Trust Premium

"Swiss-attested"

The same institutional credibility that backs Swiss banking, Swiss watches, and Geneva arbitration — applied to proving human oversight in the AI age.

Infrastructure

Hydropower grid, ETH Zürich, EPFL, Crypto Valley Zug, and an established fintech ecosystem. Switzerland has every structural advantage for building AI trust infrastructure.
