Independent Transaction Sealing for Banks and Solana Seeker

AlpenSign turns the Seeker into a Mobile HSM. By leveraging TEEPIN and Seed Vault, banks can finally provide irrefutable proof of Visa/Mastercard 3D-Secure intent directly from the client's hardware.

ALPENSIGN APP View Roadmap

Hardware-Level Proof

Uses the Seeker's Secure Element to sign transactions. The private key never leaves the hardware, protecting against even the most sophisticated mobile malware.

Independent Evidence

Instead of relying on bank server logs, AlpenSign creates a neutral, cryptographic record on the Solana blockchain that can be verified by any third party without revealing private data.

Zero-Trust Delivery

No AlpenSign backend. The app communicates directly between your bank's API and the Solana network, ensuring that no intermediary can intercept or alter the seal.
